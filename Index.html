Introduction

<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<< <<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<

Welcome! My name is Amad Fareed, I am 20 years old and hail from Lahore, Pakistan. I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and am deeply fascinated by Artificial Intelligence and its potential impact on human society. I am also an avid history reader, like to stay updated on current global affairs, and have often found myself lost in the worlds of physics, math and philosophy as well.

In this light, I ask you, do you know any better about me? If I had detailed my whole life story above, every action... every word, would you have gained a deeper insight as to who and what I am? Well... I have spent 20 years with myself and am no closer to answering that question, so probably no. The truth of the matter is that numbers, labels, or interests don’t reveal much about one’s physical or even subjective existence. Actions do indeed speak louder but after a certain point, they too don’t offer much understanding. This is perhaps because we ourselves are not aware of our fundamental reality and the nature of conscious experience. Just a collection of atoms and particles joined together in a distinct fashion? A falling drop or a passing wave in this illusory reality called life?

Many believe that there is no attainable answer to these philosophical musings and don’t see the point of pondering over them, they instead tend to stick to an ideology or creed... for comfort, through fate. While understandable, I am of the firm belief that there is an answer. It could be in the vastness of this cold universe, in the warmth of the human heart, or in the very spirit of reality. But it does exist, a deep mystery waiting to be uncovered, and its pursuit is what drives me forward, hence my fondness for AI and the sciences, containing within them the knowledge and tools that will without doubt exponentially accelerate that cause.

Thank you for visiting my website, a small glimpse into this journey and beyond!

The Structure and Goal

<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<< <<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<

This website is dedicated to showcase core concepts directly or indirectly related to AI, based on authentic and demonstratable research. Additionally, the real world implementation of AI algorithms through various projects undertaken is also exhibited.

AI Writings

  1. Exploration of various physical, metaphysical, biological, computing and philosophical (amongst others) theories and their relation to human and artificial intelligence.
  2. Explanations of core ideas and technologies behind modern artificial intelligence, as well as its potential and limitations. Will be going through a lot of boring theory.

AI Projects

  1. Real world projects based around the premise of machine and deep learning and how to build and optimise various types of AI architectures.
  2. Will include an in-depth explanation and the rationale behind every line of code. Mostly done using python and its various libraries.

If you are just interested in learning about AI and how to practically implement it to solve real world problems, congratulations! You have arrived at the right place. If you seek something deeper, like the integration of a variety of wisdom attempting to define intelligence and the very meaning of life through the ages, maybe you will find some inspiration and value here...
